Mac N Cheese

Description

This mac and cheese recipe utilises a slow cooker to create a creamy easy meal.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese
  • Milk products
  • Eggs
  • Canned soup
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water, then drain and transfer to slow cooker.
  2. Stir in butter, seasonings and around half the cheddar.
  3. Whisp evaporated milk and eggs together, then stir into pasta.
  4. Whisk the milk and soup together, then stir into pasta.
  5. Sprinkle the remaining cheese over the pasta mix and garnish with paprika.
  6. Cook on low for 2.5-3 hours.
