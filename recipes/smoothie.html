Strawberry Mango Smoothie

Description

A tropical, refreshing smoothie thatâ€™s perfect for summer mornings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 200ml orange juice
  • 100ml yogurt (plain or vanilla)
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)

Steps

  1. Add mango, strawberries, orange juice, yogurt, and honey into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into glasses and enjoy!
