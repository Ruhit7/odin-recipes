Warm Berry Compote

Description

This is a dessert which consists of frozen berries, ice cream, and juice. It utilises a slow cooker.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups ofr frozen mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1.5 teaspoons finely grated orange zest
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water

Steps

  1. Stir frozen berries, sugar, juice and zest together in a slow cooker. Heat on high until bubbling for about 1.5 hours - ensure it is covered.
  2. Stir cornstarch and water together in a small bowl until smooth. Stir into the berry mixture and cook for a further 5-10 minutes.
  3. Serve warm or at room temperature (can add ice cream).
